Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this event?

The event aims to host a benefit concert to raise funds for local homeless shelters while providing a delightful musical experience to attendees.

How can I reserve tickets for the event?

Ticket reservations can be made through our website by filling out the form provided in the Tickets section.

Are donations accepted at the event?

Yes, donations are encouraged and appreciated. All contributions will directly benefit local homeless shelters.

Will there be any additional activities at the event?

Aside from the concert, there will be a silent auction where attendees can find great deals on various items.

Can I learn more about the musicians performing?

Certainly! Detailed information about the musicians and their performances will be available on our website soon.
